Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. If you need more help, feel free to contact us.

Is Vokab It completely free?

Yes! The core features of Vokab It are, and will always be, completely free. We may introduce premium features in the future, but the essential vocabulary-building experience is for everyone.

Which browsers are supported?

Currently, Vokab It is available for Google Chrome. We are actively working on versions for Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Sign up to be notified when they are released!

Does Vokab It collect my browsing data?

No. Your privacy is our top priority. The extension only requires permission to detect and highlight words on the pages you visit. We do not track your browsing history or collect any personal data.

How many words can I save?

You can save an unlimited number of words to your personal dictionary. Build your vocabulary without any limits!

Can I sync my vocabulary across devices?

Absolutely! When you create a Vokab It account, your vocabulary automatically syncs across all your devices. You can save a word on your laptop and see it highlighted on your phone's browser.

How accurate are the AI Generated sentences?

Our AI generates contextually appropriate sentences with high accuracy. The system is trained on extensive literature and continuously improved based on user feedback. Each sentence is designed to help you understand proper word usage in real-world contexts.
